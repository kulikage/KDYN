Company Overview

Stealth deep-tech development with practical engineering execution.

Kulicage Dynamics LLC is a North Carolina-based deep-tech R&D company focused on advanced energy conversion, plasma-assisted systems, and aerospace-oriented engineering solutions.

The company develops early-stage technologies for autonomous power, remote energy systems, and high-reliability technical applications where compactness, durability, and long operating life are critical.
